About Lakshmi Tex

Crafting Excellence Since 1995

For over two decades, Lakshmi Tex has been a beacon of quality and innovation in the textile industry. Founded in 1995, we have meticulously honed our craft to become a premier manufacturer and exporter of high-quality hosiery garments, sophisticated fashion wear, and high-performance athleisure clothing.

Our philosophy marries time-honored craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology. Within our expansive, state-of-the-art facilities, skilled artisans work in synergy with modern machinery to produce textiles of unparalleled quality. We are committed to a vision of excellence, ensuring every garment we produce not only meets but surpasses the highest international standards.

Sustainability and ethical responsibility are woven into the fabric of our operations. We are dedicated to pioneering eco-friendly manufacturing processes and upholding the welfare of our team, making us the trusted partner for conscientious brands and discerning consumers across the globe.
