History

Decades of weaving trust into every meter.

LM Mills began as a small weaving unit focused on dependable fabrics for local brands. Over time we have become an integrated textile manufacturer serving customers across multiple continents.

Our growth has always been driven by understanding what designers need and investing in technology that keeps us reliable, consistent and fast.

What we are today
  • Technology-driven mill optimized for repeatable quality.
  • Partner for fashion, home & technical textile brands.
  • Long-term, relationship-focused supplier.
  • Early adopter of automation & data-driven planning.
